Best Prompts for Creating Stunning Art

Effective prompts are crucial for image creation from text. Consider using this formula: subject + detailed description + environment + art style + lighting + image quality. For example, "Half body portrait of a beautiful blonde girl in a white dress, in the forest, oil painting art, soft natural lighting, and HD image quality.“


Moreover, the initial prompts carry greater significance. You can add parentheses around keywords to signify an increased value for that specific keyword. For instance, "(horse:1.3), a tiger in art style".
